About The Junior Breakers

The Junior Breakers have been around for a long time, and are the starting grounds for youngsters
who wish to join the main team, the Sky Breakers. The Junior Breakers hosts camps for young people
who wish to play basketball and experience coaching from the Sky Breakers coach, as well as other qualifed
professionals. It is one of the top young teams in New Zealand so come in and join!
