Sky Sport Breakers:

The Sky Breaker is the premier New Zealand basketball team, and they have a number of community programmes.
One thing they do is the Champions Programme. The Sky Breaker Coaches will vist a number of schools
and teach the students about their health and ways to keep it up, along with teaching basketball.
They also do things such as getting schools to arrange the students to vist their training facility
to experience what being a Sky Sports Breaker is all about. They also do a number of community games
where you can verse other local school teams, and whichever school made more of an impact in their communities
get rewarded. If you wish to learn more, head over to this link: Sky Sport Breakers
