The Junior Breakers is a local basketball club in New Zealand
It was founded for the purpose of finding new talents for teir main team,
the Sky Breakers. It is intended for kids from 5-12 years old to join. They
take kids of all age and ethnicities.

The Sky Breaker is the premier New Zealand basketball team, which particpates
in basketball locally and in Australia. They recruit people across New Zealand
and directly from their fledgeling team, the Junior Breakers
